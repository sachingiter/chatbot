WHO IS CONCIEDGE?

Based in London,UK, Conciedge is a technology company that has launched the first virtual assistant. With the first phase focusing on travel concierge will is a human supported chatbot that grows and adapts to the needs of its user.

Conciedge is your own personal assistant. The more you use conciedge the more conciedge learns about you.

Conciedge is with you wherever you are 24hrs a day. You can start a conversation or request directly from your smartphone.

This is a human supported, 24/7 chatbot that is available as an app on your mobile device. Wherever your mobile device goes, Conciedge is ready to offer you tailored options quickly.
